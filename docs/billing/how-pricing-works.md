# How Pricing Works

Therativities uses a simple pay-as-you-go model.

## Per-session fee

You pay a small platform fee per session. There are no monthly subscriptions or hidden costs.

## Stripe processing fees

Standard Stripe processing fees apply when collecting payments from parents. These are separate from the Therativities platform fee.
