# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Therativities?

Therativities is a practice management platform built for pediatric therapists (OT, PT, SLP, behavioral, ABA). It helps you manage clients, schedule sessions, write documentation, collect payments, and share progress with parents.

### Who is Therativities for?

Primarily private-pay pediatric therapists and small therapy practices. Whether you work solo or with a small team, Therativities is designed for you.

### Is Therativities HIPAA compliant?

Therativities is designed for HIPAA compliance with encryption, role-based access, and secure infrastructure. We are actively working toward full formal compliance including BAA execution. See our [HIPAA Compliance page](https://app.therativities.com/hipaa-compliance) for details.

## Billing

### How much does Therativities cost?

Therativities uses a pay-as-you-go model with a small per-session platform fee. There are no monthly subscriptions.

### Are there any hidden fees?

No. You pay the per-session platform fee plus standard Stripe processing fees when collecting payments.

## Data & Privacy

### Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Auth0 for authentication and Convex for our backend, both of which provide enterprise-grade security.

### Do you sell my data?

No. We never sell your data or share it for third-party advertising. See our [Privacy Policy](https://app.therativities.com/privacy-policy).

### Is my data used to train AI?

No. Your data is never used to train AI models.

## Support

### How do I get help?

Email us at [support@therativities.com](mailto:support@therativities.com). We typically respond within one business day.

### How do I request a feature?

Submit a feature request directly in the app at [Feature Requests](https://app.therativities.com/app/feature-requests).
