# Roadmap & Feature Requests

We build Therativities in the open. You can see what we're working on, what's coming next, and tell us what you need.

## Roadmap

View our product roadmap at [app.therativities.com/app/roadmap](https://app.therativities.com/app/roadmap). It shows features that are:
- **Planned** — On our list to build
- **In Progress** — Currently being developed
- **Completed** — Recently shipped

## Feature requests

Have an idea for something that would make your practice better? Submit it at [app.therativities.com/app/feature-requests](https://app.therativities.com/app/feature-requests).

We review every request and use them to prioritize what we build next. You can also vote on other users' requests.

## Changelog

See what's new and what's changed at [app.therativities.com/app/changelog](https://app.therativities.com/app/changelog). We post updates whenever we ship new features, improvements, or fixes.
