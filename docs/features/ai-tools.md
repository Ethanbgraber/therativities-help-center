# AI Tools

Therativities includes AI-powered tools to help you work more efficiently.

## AI-assisted session notes

Generate session note drafts based on your session details. You always have full control to review and edit before saving.

## How your data is used

- AI features are powered by OpenAI
- Your data is **never** used to train AI models
- We **never** sell your data to third parties
- See our [Privacy Policy](https://app.therativities.com/privacy-policy) for full details
