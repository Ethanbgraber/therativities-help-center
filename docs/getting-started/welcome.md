# Welcome to Therativities

Therativities is a practice management platform built specifically for pediatric therapists — occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, physical therapists, behavioral therapists, and ABA providers.

## What you can do with Therativities

- **Manage clients** — Keep all client information, goals, and progress in one place
- **Schedule sessions** — Book, reschedule, and track appointments
- **Write documentation** — Create session notes with AI-assisted tools
- **Collect payments** — Process private-pay payments seamlessly through Stripe
- **Share with parents** — Give parents access to their child's progress through the Parent Portal
- **Track your practice** — View key metrics on your dashboard
