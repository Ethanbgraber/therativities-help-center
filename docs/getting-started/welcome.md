# Welcome to Therativities

Therativities is a practice management platform built specifically for pediatric therapists — occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, physical therapists, behavioral therapists, ABA providers, and more.

Whether you work solo or with a small team, Therativities helps you run your private-pay practice from one place.

## What you can do

- **Manage clients** — Store client profiles, guardian info, diagnoses, and clinical details
- **Schedule sessions** — Book one-time or recurring appointments with conflict detection
- **Write documentation** — Draft session notes, generate SOAP notes with AI, and create parent summaries
- **Generate activities** — Use AI to plan session activities based on goals, materials, and child interests
- **Collect payments** — Process private-pay payments through Stripe, or record cash/check/Zelle/Venmo payments
- **Share with parents** — Invite parents to view their child's sessions, progress, and billing through the Parent Portal
- **Track your practice** — See today's sessions, weekly stats, and billing at a glance on your dashboard
- **Create evaluations** — Build evaluation reports with AI assistance and customizable templates
