# Setting Up Your Practice

After creating your account, you'll need to configure your practice details.

## Practice profile

- Practice name
- Your specialty (OT, PT, SLP, behavioral, ABA)
- Contact information
- Business address

## Adding your first client

Once your practice is set up, you can start adding clients. See [Client Management](/features/client-management) for a full guide.
