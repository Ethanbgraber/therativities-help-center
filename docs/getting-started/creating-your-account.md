# Creating Your Account

Get started with Therativities in just a few minutes.

## Sign up

1. Go to [app.therativities.com](https://app.therativities.com)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email

## What happens next

After signing up, you'll be guided through setting up your practice profile. See [Setting Up Your Practice](./setting-up-your-practice) for details.
