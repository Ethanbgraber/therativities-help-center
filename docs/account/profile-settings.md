# Profile & Settings

All practice configuration lives under **Settings** (gear icon in the navigation). Here's what each section covers.

## Profile tab

### Basic information
- **Name** — Your display name
- **Phone Number** — Auto-formatted as you type

### Professional licenses
Manage your state licenses:
- **License Number** (required)
- **License State** (required — dropdown of all 50 states)
- **Online Record Link** (optional — link to your state licensing board)

You can add multiple licenses if you're licensed in more than one state.

### Education
Track your degrees and certifications:
- **Degree Name**
- **Level** — Associate, Bachelor's, Master's, Doctorate, PhD, Certificate, or Diploma
- **Institution**
- **Year**

### Professional details
- **Professional Credentials** — e.g., OTR/L, PT, DPT, SLP, BCBA
- **Years of Experience**
- **Specialties** — Multi-select from the same list you chose during onboarding
- **Professional Bio** — Free-text description of your practice and approach

### Business information
- **NPI Number** — Required for super bills
- **TIN Number** — Required for super bills
- **Practice Address** — Street, City, State, ZIP

### Provider signature
Draw your signature on the signature pad. This is used on documents and super bills. An audit trail records your device info and timestamp automatically.

## Account tab

- Email and password management
- Account actions (logout, etc.)
- Danger zone (account deletion)

## Calendar tab

Calendar display preferences — customize how your calendar looks and behaves.

## Notifications tab

Control which notifications you receive and how (push notifications, email).

## Privacy & Security tab

- Privacy settings for how client information is displayed
- **Security PIN** — Set or update your app PIN

## Scheduling Rules tab

Configure your availability and scheduling behavior across multiple levels:

- **Therapist** — Your personal availability, buffer times between sessions, cancellation policy, holidays, vacations, and timezone
- **Institution** — Hours and holidays for schools or facilities you work at
- **Room** — Room-specific hours and exceptions
- **Client** — Client-specific scheduling rules

## Sessions tab

- **Materials** — Create and manage material bags (collections of therapy materials). Mark materials as "always brought" so they appear in every session's AI activity generation.
- **Structure** — Create session templates with segments (e.g., Warm-up, Main Activity, Cool-down), durations, and guidelines.

## Documents tab

Manage your document library — templates, forms, and other files.

## Evaluations tab

Configure evaluation templates and intake forms for different disciplines.

## Organizations & Access tab

Manage your practice entities, team members, and access controls. See [Team Management](/account/team-management) for details.

## Billing tab

- **Payment methods** — Your card on file for platform fees
- **Stripe account** — Connect or manage your Stripe Connect account
- **Services** — Session and evaluation services with pricing
- **Billing frequency** — Default billing schedule
- **Fee policy** — How processing fees are handled
